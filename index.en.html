About the event

The conference on “FabLabs and Learning” will take place on July 12th-15th 2018 at Bataville, France. It is just before FAB14 which is the international FabLab conference that will be held at Toulouse on July 16th-20th. Surrounded by an amazing landscape, our goal is to discuss and explore on the following questions:

FAB14 ‐ France will have three main events:

Fab City Summit
will take place on July 12th-13th 2018 in Paris. It is organized by the "Fab City Grand Paris" association.
Fab14 Distributed
will be on July 14th-15th throughout different french cities, and supported by the French Fablab Network (RFFLabs), where FabEdu is being part of.
Fab14 Conference & Symposium
on July 16th-20th in Toulouse.
Fab Festival
will take place on July 21th-22th at Toulouse supported by Artilect.

Programme

The progamme is beign made.. Comming soon!

#STEAM:
What is the role of an object created in a Fablab? Is it useful in the learning process?
#FabLabSchool:
What are the events, tools (e.g. machines, software/hardware), physical space that can foster the learning process in an educational third place?
#LearningTomorrow:
How the FabLabs could promote a learning society?
#Inclusion:
What could be the innovative pedagogies in a FabLab in order to favour diversity and inclusivity?
#OpenBadge:
How to recognize the accomplishments and skills developed in a FabLab?
#FabLabUbiquity:
How a mobile FabLab could propose (or better) new learning practices in a territory?
